Mounts

Complete list of mounts in Ascalon.

Mount Name Description
Black Sheep Obtained by completing Orc Warzone Quest.
Blazebringer Available in Premium Shop for 100 points.
Cave Tarantula Kill a Cave Tarantula while riding the Black Sheep.
Coral Rhea Use Magical Horseshoe item awarded randomly by winning Events.
Crystal Wolf Use Heart of a Crystal Wolf item obtained in Froza Dungeon.
Doombringer Reward from Dreamhaunters Quest.
Dragonling Obtainable from dungeon token levers for 50 Dungeon Tokens.
Draptor Use Reins on a Draptor (It has 25% chance to success) Item also droped by Draptor.
Dromedary Use Magical Mount Potion obtained in Oken Dungeon.
Dusk Pryer NPC Siuan daily mission reward chance.
Eventide Nandu Use Magical Crystal Ball rare Grove Keepers loot.
Ebony Tiger First: Go to Hideous Fungus and Look the Druid Statue then use it, you will be teleported to NPC Oran Stonebloom.
Second: NPC Oran Stonebloom: Kill 100 Hideous Fungus.
Third: NPC Oran Stonebloom: Find and click Druid Statue in Gargoyles then go back to NPC. (You now gained access to Dark Hydras)
Fourth: NPC Oran Stonebloom: Find and click the Magical Drawer in Dark Hydras (West of Pirates) then go back to Hideous Fungus and go downstairs to get your reward.
Emerald Rune Watcher Equip Phoenix in the weapon slot and use chest in Rune Watchers to get it.
Gigantica Theres 1% chance to tame after killing a Gigantica.
Hibernal Moth Use Magical Drink item droped by King Granirk Boss.
Kingly Deer Use Magical Mount Potion item obtained in Toxic Dungeon.
Lady Bug Use item in Lady Bug with a % chance of taming. (droped by Ladybug, appears in Elves Raid)
Lava Monkey Use Morgatha's Brain item obtained in Hellfire Dungeon.
Manta Ray Use Harness on a Manta Ray (It has 40% chance to success). Item also droped by Manta Ray.
Midnight Panther Reward from Toxic Bugs Quest.
Mutated Rat Obtainable from Grappler task.
First: NPC Rubra: Kill 450 Grapplers.
Second NPC Rubra: Get 30 Beholder Eyes and 50 Unique Toes.
Panda NPC Dungeon Keeper: Get 30 Nettle Blossoms looted from Insectoid Raid in Swamplands.
Plumfish Use Northern Fishburger item, this item can be obtained while fishing, you need something like skill 70.
Prismatic Unicorn Awarded randomly by winning Events.
Racing Bird NPC Aviendha: Get a Silver Brooch to unlock this mount.
Rapid Boar NPC Aviendha: Get a Silver Brooch to unlock this mount.
Savanna Ostrich Can be obtained in a chest in Hydras Spawn.
Scorpion King Available in Premium Shop for 200 points.
Shadow Draptor Available in Premium Shop for 200 points.
Snowman Theres a chance to obtain it by killing Snowmen in Frosthaven.
Stampor NPC Chondur: Get 15 stampor horns, 15 stampor talons and 15 hollow stampor hoofs.
Steelbeak NPC Aila: Get 50 Steelbeak Horns.
Stone Grappler (Hellgrip) Use Blue Note item droped by Pharioah Boss.
Tarantula Use Tarantula Egg item droped by Lady Xiania Boss.
Tin Lizzard Reward from Succubus Den Quest.
Undead Cavebear Use Maxilla Maximus item on a Undead Cavebear to tame it. (It has 25% chance to success) Item also droped by Undead Cavebears.
War Bear NPC Aviendha: Get a Silver Brooch to unlock this mount.
Widow Queen Use Sweet Smelling Bait item on a Wailing Widow to tame it. (It has 40% chance to success) Item droped by Widow Queen Boss.
Winter Wyvern Obtainable from Ascalon Token Levers. Cost: 30 Ascalon Tokens.
Young Red Dragon Theres a chance to obtain it by killing Dragon Tamers.
